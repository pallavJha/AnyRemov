Host Identifier(';' Separated)
1. Invaild hostname is present. Sample hostnames are google.com or images.saasworthy.com
2. Make sure that the multiple Identifiers are separated by a semicolon. To get an identifier, open the Elements Section present in the Inspect panel(F12), right click on the element(which you want to hide), click on the `Copy Selector` present in the `Copy` Option. The selector text will be automatically copied to the clipboard.
